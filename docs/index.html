Project Research Question

"How do Manhattan apartment prices respond to macroeconomic conditions across different economic regimes, and does incorporating textual sentiment from FOMC communications improve predictive accuracy?"

Conclusion: Yes. While regimes alone don't shift baseline pricing globally, the interaction between regimes and specific housing segments (e.g., 2-bedrooms in TriBeCa) is highly significant. Adding Regimes and FOMC Sentiment to Random Forest models outperformed baseline macroeconomic models.
